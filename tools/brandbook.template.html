03

Typography

Five faces, each with one clear job. Serif display for voice, two sans for body (one per app-zone), a mono for structure, and a geometric sans for the wordmark alone. A quirk to remember: the font-fraunces and font-inter token names are legacy aliases — they now render Playfair and Figtree respectively.

04

Liquid glass

Surfaces are translucent white panes that blur whatever pastel light sits behind them. Cards float (lit edge + drop shadow); buttons sit flush (flat-on-glass, no border). Below is the real recipe, rendered live over a blob field — the accent pill is the single "lead the eye" action.

Frosted card

rgba(255,255,255,.50) tint · blur(16px) saturate(1.7)

keyword portrait

.glass-card tint .50 / blur 16 · .glass-card-frosted tint .62 / blur 24 · .glass-btn white .28 (flush) · .btn-glass-primary white .64 (floats) · .glass-btn-accent orange .92 + cream text · .glass-header white .46 / blur 22. Background blobs & grain are static — no per-frame compositing (ADR 0019), since every glass pane already backdrop-blurs the layer behind it.

05

Where it lives

Single sources of truth, so the brandbook never drifts from the code.

src/app/globals.css
All color tokens (:root + @theme), glass recipes, gradients, blobs, the global serif h1/h2/h3 rule.
src/app/layout.tsx
All five fonts loaded & wired to CSS variables (Geist, Geist Mono, Playfair, Outfit, Figtree).
src/components/Logo.tsx
Wordmark lockup — inline SVG, Outfit text, coral-to-magenta gradient, light/dark prop.
src/app/brand-artifacts/
Canonical SVG assets: logo-lockup, logo-mark, watermark (+ icon.svg favicon).
src/components/blobConfig.ts
Background blob palettes — coral-lilac in production; teal/amber/mono defined but unused.
